Welcome to our Online Hearing Test, a quick way to gauge how well you’re hearing.

In only 3 minutes, you can test your ability to distinguish certain words and numbers in a noisy environment. We recommend taking the test in a quiet area without interruptions. You will have the option to take the hearing test using your device speakers or headphones.

About Us

The "HearCheck" is a web application that performs Hearing Tests such as Pure-Tone Audiometry and Speech Audiometry, ensuring a thorough assessment of users' auditory health. Our application emphasizes the principle "precaution is better than cure," enabling individuals of all ages, from children to the elderly, to proactively perform regular basic hearing tests on their personal devices.

How the test works

This hearing test will help you identify difficulties with understanding speech in noise.

  • First, you will hear different combinations of words, and then numbers, always in groups of three, while background noise varies.
  • Select the three things you hear by selecting the corresponding icon or number shown to the left.
  • You have to select the three things in the same order you hear them.
  • Finally, if you are using hearing aids, we recommend that you remove them while taking the test.
